How it works

Import Process

Importing a vehicle doesn't need to be complicated. At Tokyo Select, we've simplified the process into clear, transparent steps — keeping you informed from start to finish.

01
Browse or Request

Choose from our available stock or request a quote for a similar unit based on your preferred make, model, year, and budget.

02
Get a Quote

We provide a full landed cost estimate covering:

  • Vehicle price
  • Shipping & logistics
  • Import duties and taxes
  • Inspection and clearance fees
No hidden costs. No surprises.
03
Commit

Once you're comfortable with the quote, a deposit is paid to get the process started. We then share available vehicles for your approval before purchase.

04
Purchase & Shipping

After approval:

  • The vehicle is purchased
  • Export documentation is prepared
  • The vehicle is shipped to Kenya
You'll receive regular updates throughout this stage.
05
Clearance & Registration

On arrival:

  • Port clearance is handled
  • Mandatory inspections are completed
  • Duties and taxes are paid as assessed by KRA
  • Vehicle registration is processed
06
Delivery

Once cleared and registered, the vehicle is delivered to you — ready for handover.

What You Can Expect
  • Verified vehicle sourcing
  • Transparent pricing
  • Regular progress updates
  • Professional handling at every stage
